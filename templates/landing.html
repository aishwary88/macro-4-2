Core Capabilities

Everything you need for traffic intelligence

A complete pipeline from raw video to actionable insights, powered by YOLOv8 and advanced tracking algorithms.

Video & Live Camera Analysis

Upload pre-recorded footage or connect a live camera stream. Supports MP4, AVI, MOV, MKV formats up to 2GB, plus IP camera URLs and local webcams.

MP4 / AVI / MOV IP Camera Webcam

Speed Estimation

Calibrated speed measurement using perspective transformation and frame-rate analysis. Configurable speed thresholds for overspeed detection.

License Plate Recognition

Automatic Number Plate Recognition (ANPR) extracts and logs plate numbers for every tracked vehicle using OCR and image enhancement.

Vehicle Classification

Classifies detected objects into Cars, Trucks, Buses, and Bikes using YOLOv8 with per-class counts and statistics in the results panel.

Real-Time Analytics

Live dashboard updates with total vehicle count, average speed, overspeed events, and per-class breakdowns. All data persisted to SQLite.

Excel Report Export

Download a structured Excel report with per-vehicle data including ID, type, plate number, average speed, max speed, and overspeed status.
